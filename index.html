Dishwasher Court

The dishwasher is loaded. The court is in session.

Dishwasher Court is a playful puzzle game where every mug, pan, keyboard, and deeply questionable life choice must be fitted into the machine. Build a load, hit wash, and receive the court's verdict.

Get Support Privacy Policy

What kind of game is it?

It's a compact logic-and-layout puzzle game with a comedy voice. Every level gives you a dishwasher, a pile of items, and just enough space to make you doubt your choices.

What makes it fun?

  • Tight spatial puzzles with rotating, stacking, and rack-specific rules
  • Absurd objects like phones, keyboards, statues, and other terrible ideas
  • Verdicts that turn every wash cycle into a tiny courtroom comedy

Who is it for?

Anyone who likes smart puzzle games, household chaos, and the special feeling of being judged by a dishwasher.
