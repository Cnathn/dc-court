Dishwasher Court

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Dishwasher Court is designed to be playable without creating an account or providing your name, address, or profile information directly to us.

Information we collect

We do not directly ask you to create an account or submit personal profile information to use the app.

The app may store gameplay-related information on your device, such as unlocked progress, local settings, and best scores, so the game can function properly.

Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase, that transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store, not by us directly. We do not receive your full payment details.

Advertising and consent

The app includes support for advertising and privacy-consent technology. If ads or consent flows are used in a released version of the app, those services may process information as described in their own policies and as permitted by your device and regional privacy settings.

Analytics and diagnostics

We do not sell your personal information. Standard diagnostic, crash, or device information may be collected by Apple or other platform services you use, subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

Children's privacy

Dishwasher Court is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can stop using the app at any time and remove locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact dishwashercourtapp@seznam.cz.
