Dishwasher Court

Support

If the dishwasher misbehaves, the court will hear your case.

Contact

Email support at dishwashercourtapp@seznam.cz.

When reporting a bug, it helps to include:

  • your device model
  • your iOS version
  • the level or case number
  • what you expected to happen
  • a screenshot if the issue is visual

Common reasons to write in

  • a level appears impossible
  • an item does not snap where it should
  • the layout looks broken on your device
  • you need help with a purchase or restore flow
